Operating principle

Keep the $80K Full-Time Job as Runway

The full-time job is the funding engine. Bridge roles should add cash, tech exposure, proof, and network without burning down family time or the current income base.

Take

Part-time or flexible roles that expose you to tickets, users, hardware, systems, automation, infrastructure, or insurance technology.

Avoid

Low-value second jobs that only consume evenings and weekends without improving technical credibility or transition leverage.

Ask

Will this role create resume evidence, useful contacts, realistic learning reps, and an exit path into a better tech role?

Target order

Best Lanes for This Transition

  1. Part-time IT SupportFastest credible tech proof while keeping income stable.
  2. Data Center / InfrastructureGood fit for homelab, server hardware, cabling, and uptime mindset.
  3. Automation / SystemsUses Python, Flask, dashboards, CSV processing, and workflow automation.
  4. Insurtech / Insurance SystemsCombines 15+ years insurance operations with technical systems work.
  5. Freelance Small Business AutomationUseful side proof if scoped tightly and protected from time creep.

Decision rule

Bridge Role Test

  • Cash does not need to replace the full-time job.
  • Schedule must protect sleep, family time, and job performance.
  • Tech exposure must be specific enough to become resume bullets.
  • People fit matters because the network is part of the payoff.
  • Stress cost must be treated as a real negative score.
